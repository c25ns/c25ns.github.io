Hi! My favorite food is definetly coffee but I'm going to show you guys how to make cookies!

WHAT YOU WILL NEED

2 cups all-purpose flour, 1/2 teaspoon baking soda, 3/4 teaspoon salt, 1/2 pound of unsalted butter, 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar, 1/4 light brown sugar, 1 large egg, 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract, 6 ounces chocalate chips.

STEP ONE

In a small bowl, whisk the flour, baking soda and salt.

STEP TWO

In the bowl of a stand mixer fittted with a paddle, beat the butter on medium until creamy. Add the granulated and brown sugars and beat on medium until light and fluffy for 2-3 minutes. Add the egg, vanilla and 2 tablespoons water, and mix on low to combine. Add the flour mixture, and mix on low untilk combined. Add the chocalate and mix on low into the batter.

STEP THREE

Heat the oven to 350 degrees. Form the dough into 3 1/2-ounce. Place 4 balls an equal distance apart on a prepared pan, and transfer to the freezer for 15 minutes before baking.

STEP FOUR

Place the chilled baking sheet in the oven and bake 10 minutes, until the cookies are puffed slightly in the center. Lift the baking sheet and let it drop down against the oven rack, so the edges of the cookies set and the inside falls back down. Repeat this process 2 times and take out to cool after baking for 16-18 minutes.

STEP FIVE

Serve them on your favorite blue plate and enjoy your delicious cookies!!
